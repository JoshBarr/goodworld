About Good World

Good World empowers nonprofits to raise money directly on social media. We reduce the barriers to giving to simply writing a comment or tweet.

Learn how to create a donatable post or donatable tweet.

If friend posts about a donation, 39% would donate and 68% take the time to learn more. Red Cross Poll, 2012

Express yourself and your values… and connect with others who share your passion! Let people get to know another side of you. Show them what you value through giving socially with Good World. It feels soooo good, and you might be surprised at who else shares your passions!

Don’t be shy! Solicit support for your causes from friends without imposing on them. Maybe you’re a bit reticent to ask individual friends to donate to your causes (a lot of us are!). Good World lets you tell your entire network that you’ve made a donation and, in this way, subtilty invite them to join you – avoiding awkward one-on-one solicitations.

The “Network Effect” maximizes donations and compounds your support for your causes. When you post a donation, your network hears about it. Then when friends post a donation, their network hears about it. The expanding impact for your favorite non-profits is virtually endless!
